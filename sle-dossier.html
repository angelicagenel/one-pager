What SLE Makes Possible

For Your Students

Students step into a living world: the Oaxacan feria, its vendors, its legends, its language. Each lesson is a scene that builds real communicative skill.

For Your Program

Built on ACTFL proficiency guidelines and second language acquisition research. Six skill areas. A complete scope and sequence. Designed to take students from first greetings to confident expression across three courses.

For Your Institution

One institutional package, ready to deploy each term. Students access a complete digital learning system at no additional cost. No codes. No subscriptions. No out-of-pocket fees.

The SLE System

Every SLE lesson covers a complete communicative skill, grounded in Mexican cultural context and designed for flipped instruction. Grammar is prepared before class. Class time is for people, conversation, and connection.

  • 1 Vocabulary
  • 2 Grammar & Verb Patterns
  • 3 Connectors & Text Organization
  • 4 Pronunciation & Writing
  • 5 Context, Culture & Appropriateness
  • 6 Communication Strategies

Integrated AI speech practice: FACT (Functional Acoustic-Communicative Tracking) gives students real-time feedback on clarity and communication directly inside each lesson.

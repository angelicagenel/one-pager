Course Sequence

View full lesson outline →

Unit 1 Hola, soy yo
SER tú/usted hay articles nouns

L01–L04 + CT1

  • I can introduce myself and ask about others.
  • I can describe what exists in a space.
  • I can recognize Mexican greeting practices.
Unit 2 El mundo que te rodea
Adjectives SER + adjective possessives family vocab

L05–L09 + CT2

  • I can describe people and objects using adjectives.
  • I can talk about what belongs to me and others.
  • I can introduce and describe my family.
Unit 3 La vida en movimiento
-AR/-ER/-IR verbs connectors

L10–L14 + CT3

  • I can describe my daily routine and activities.
  • I can ask and answer questions about actions.
  • I can organize a sequence of events using connectors.
Unit 4 Tiempo, números y agenda
La hora TENER numbers 0–100 schedules

L15–L19 + CT4

  • I can tell time and describe my schedule.
  • I can express what I have and need.
  • I can count and use numbers 0–100.
Unit 5 Aquí y ahora
ESTAR IR + a VENIR progressive

L20–L24 + CT5

  • I can describe where things and people are.
  • I can express emotions and temporary states.
  • I can say where I am going and what is happening right now.
AC Alcance Completo: Extended Grammar Track
Stem-changers irregular yo SER/ESTAR DOP

AC1–AC4 · Optional

  • I can use a wider range of verb forms.
  • I can express more complex ideas with precision.

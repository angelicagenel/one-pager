Can-Do Goal

I can introduce myself, say where I'm from, tell time, and share basic personal information.

Vocabulary
Greetings & farewells Names & nationalities Numbers 0–30 Identity words Telling time
Grammar Structures
Nouns & articles Present tense of ser Expressing time Forming basic questions
Can-Do Goal

I can describe places in my city and neighborhood and ask where things are or what people do there.

Vocabulary
City & neighborhood Buildings & places Numbers 31–100 Streets & transit
Grammar Structures
Regular –ar verbs Present tense of estar Question formation Interrogative words
Can-Do Goal

I can talk about my family, describe where I live, and explain what roles people have at home and at work.

Vocabulary
Family members Rooms of the house Household roles Work & occupations
Grammar Structures
Descriptive adjectives Possessive adjectives Regular –er / –ir verbs Tener & venir Tener que + infinitive
Can-Do Goal

I can say what I do on weekends, talk about obligations, and express future plans and habitual actions.

Vocabulary
Leisure & weekend activities Obligations & schedules Places & transport
Grammar Structures
Present tense of ir Ir + a + infinitive Stem-changing verbs (e→ie, o→ue, e→i) Irregular yo forms

I can talk about a trip, describe how I feel, compare places and experiences, and use saber and conocer correctly.

Vocabulary
Travel & vacation Feelings & emotions Destinations Cultural comparisons
Grammar Structures
Estar + emotions & conditions Present progressive Ser vs. estar (full contrast) Saber vs. conocer Direct object pronouns

Course Sequence

Six thematic clusters, each building on the last.

Cluster 1 Shopping & Transactions
Regular preterite indirect object pronouns demonstratives

Markets, prices, and purchases in Mexican cultural context.

  • I can narrate a purchase or transaction.
  • I can explain what I bought and for whom.
Cluster 2 Daily Routines & Habits
Reflexive verbs indefinite/negative words preterite of SER/IR

Personal care, habits, and cultural routines.

  • I can describe my daily routine in detail.
  • I can talk about habits and personal care.
Cluster 3 Food, Celebrations & Experiences
Gustar impersonal/passive se comparatives

Food, social meals, and Mexican celebrations.

  • I can recount a celebration or meal experience.
  • I can express preferences and make comparisons.
Cluster 4 Health & Wellbeing
Stem-changing preterites double object pronouns tener que

Health situations and medical contexts in Mexico.

  • I can explain symptoms and health situations.
  • I can describe obligations and habits.
Cluster 5 Past Narration
Irregular preterites preterite vs. imperfect contrast

Telling stories and recounting experiences.

  • I can narrate a past experience with logical sequence.
  • I can distinguish main events from background context.
Cluster 6 Describing & Comparing
Comparatives relative pronouns imperfect a personal

Describing people, clothing, and experiences in detail.

  • I can describe and compare people and experiences.
  • I can build longer sentences using relative clauses.

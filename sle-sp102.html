Course Sequence

Click any lesson to see vocabulary, grammar, and can-do goals.

Can-Do Goal

I can explain what I bought, for whom, and describe objects using numbers, prices, and demonstrative adjectives.

Vocabulary
Shopping & markets Numbers 101+ Products & prices Bargaining & deals
Grammar Structures
Preterite of regular verbs Indirect object pronouns Demonstrative adjectives Saber vs. conocer
Can-Do Goal

I can talk about past routines, describe what I liked or disliked, and use reflexive verbs to talk about daily self-care.

Vocabulary
Daily routine & self-care Personal habits Cultural practices
Grammar Structures
Reflexive verbs Indefinite & negative words Preterite of ser / ir Gustar & similar verbs
Can-Do Goal

I can recount a meal experience, compare dishes, and express how something tasted or felt to me using past tense narration.

Vocabulary
Food & restaurant Gastronomy & culture Social dining
Grammar Structures
Preterite of stem-changing verbs Double object pronouns Comparatives & superlatives
Can-Do Goal

I can recount a celebration, explain reasons for actions, and connect ideas using relative clauses and por / para.

Vocabulary
Celebrations & traditions Mexican cultural events Reasons & causes
Grammar Structures
Irregular preterites Verbs that change meaning in preterite Por vs. para Relative pronouns (que, quien, lo que)
Can-Do Goal

I can narrate past habits, describe symptoms, and explain general health practices using the imperfect and se constructions.

Vocabulary
Health & well-being Symptoms & medical visits Wellness habits
Grammar Structures
Imperfect tense Preterite vs. imperfect (contrast) Impersonal & passive se Adverbs of manner & frequency

Course Sequence

Click any lesson to see vocabulary, grammar, and can-do goals.

Can-Do Goal

I can give instructions, recommend apps and services, and discuss the role of technology in daily urban life — distinguishing informal and formal registers.

Vocabulary
Technology & digital media Urban services & transit Social media & communication
Grammar Structures
Familiar affirmative & negative commands Por vs. para Reciprocal pronouns
Can-Do Goal

I can negotiate household responsibilities, express rules and expectations using formal commands, and describe complex domestic routines.

Vocabulary
Home & household tasks Obligations & agreements Shared spaces & roles
Grammar Structures
Formal commands (usted / ustedes) Nosotros commands Advanced relative pronouns
Can-Do Goal

I can express emotions and doubts about environmental issues, react to uncertain information, and evaluate situations from my own perspective.

Vocabulary
Environment & nature Conservation & sustainability Climate & geography
Grammar Structures
Subjunctive — emotion, doubt, negation Subjunctive in adjective clauses Subjunctive vs. indicative (contrast)
Can-Do Goal

I can project plans for personal wellness, express hypothetical situations, and give evidence-based advice about health and lifestyle choices.

Vocabulary
Exercise & nutrition Mental health & balance Medical & wellness services
Grammar Structures
Simple future Conditional simple Present perfect
Can-Do Goal

I can argue a position on cultural values, express organized opinions using discourse markers, and reflect on personal and collective identity in Spanish-speaking contexts.

Vocabulary
Identity & cultural values Social thought & community Mexican cultural perspectives
Grammar Structures
Advanced discourse markers (sin embargo, por lo tanto, a pesar de) Condition & consequence (si… entonces) Cause & purpose connectors

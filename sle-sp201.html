Course Sequence

Five thematic clusters building toward argumentative expression.

Cluster 1 Instructions & Regulation
Affirmative/negative commands formal commands por vs. para reciprocal se

Giving instructions and navigating social norms.

  • I can give instructions and recommendations.
  • I can adapt my register to the social situation.
Cluster 2 Feelings & Reactions
Present subjunctive emotion doubt negation

Expressing subjectivity, emotion, and nuanced opinion.

  • I can express emotions and doubts using the subjunctive.
  • I can nuance my opinions grammatically.
Cluster 3 Future Plans & Possibilities
Simple future conditional imperfect subjunctive

Projecting plans, possibilities, and hypothetical situations.

  • I can talk about future plans and hypothetical situations.
  • I can express possibility and give evidence-based recommendations.
Cluster 4 Experience & Reflection
Present perfect nosotros commands advanced discourse markers

Reflecting on accumulated experience and arguing a position.

  • I can reflect on accumulated experiences.
  • I can argue a position using organized discourse.
Cluster 5 Opinion & Argument
Advanced relative pronouns subjunctive vs. indicative

Taking a stance on social and cultural topics.

  • I can express well-supported opinions on social and cultural topics.
  • I can write and speak argumentatively.
